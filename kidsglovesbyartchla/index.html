Our Story

Our founder saw that the kids in her grade school class were masters at mess making. This expertise caused much consternation with the parents when one teacher cannot take 15 minutes to wash every kids hands before school is over. This got her thinking on how to run her class smooth, keep parents happy, and still manage many fun (and messy) activites. Add the health concern of snack time and a remedied is mandatory.
Enter Artchla gloves, the peel and throw away clean-up.
